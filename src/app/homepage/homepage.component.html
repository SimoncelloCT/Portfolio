About me

Hello! I'm Simone Scionti. I'm a curious and disciplined software engineer with a deep interest in Apple technologies.
I'm proficient in iOS native mobile development and have knowledge about cloud serverless architectures on AWS.
With a keen eye for design patterns, I adhere to industry best practices maximizing reuse and promoting maintainability.
I’m always seeking ways to deliver an extraordinary experience to the end-users, I strive to write well-structured code, work in a team and mentor other people.

Later I decided to follow my passion for mobile development by consolidating my knowledge about the Apple ecosystem and approaching multi-platform mobile technologies such as Flutter.
Recently I started my PhD on Artificial Intelligence applied to cultural heritage, a discipline I’m curious about.
